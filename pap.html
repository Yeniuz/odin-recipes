Pap

description

It is made of ground maize and water

A favourite of All South Africans!

Ingridients

  • 3 cups water
  • 2 cups maize meal

Steps to cook

  1. In a pot bring 3 cups water to a rapid boil
  2. Add half the maize meal and cover. Reduce the heat to medium-low and leave the pap for 8 to 10 minutes.
  3. Repeatedly mash the pap against the side of the pot for approximately 2 minutes to get rid of any lumps
  4. Repeatedly mash the pap against the side of the saucepan for approximately 2 minutes to get rid of any lumps
