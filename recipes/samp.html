Samp

Description

It is made of de-hulled dried corn and dried beans

Ingridients

  • 200g samp
  • Sea salt
  • 200g dried black beans
  • Water

Steps to cook

  1. Soak the beans in water overnight.
  2. Discard the water, Rinse the beans
  3. Place in a large pot with the samp.
  4. Add the water and simmer for 2 hours
