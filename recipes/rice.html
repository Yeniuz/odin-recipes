Rice

Description

It is made of rice granules and water

A favourite of Asian people!

Ingridients

  • 300g sushi rice
  • 1/3 cup rice vinegar
  • 4 cups cold water
  • 1 T salt

Steps to cook

  1. Place the sushi rice into a fine colander and run cold water through it for 2 to 3 minutes, until the water runs clear.
  2. place in a heavy-bottomed saucepan with the washed sushi rice and cold water. Cover and set aside to soak for about 35 minutes.
  3. Bring to a boil over a medium to high heat then reduce the heat and allow the rice to gently simmer, covered, for 15 minutes or until cooked, stirring now and again to ensure the bottom doesn't catch
  4. Remove from the heat and allow to steam, still covered, for a further 15 minutes.
